# PMXT Feb 22-27 Spike Report

**Date:** 2026-03-27  
**Backtest window:** 2026-02-22 to 2026-02-27  
**Database:** isolated `polyarb_backtest` only  
**Scope:** pair-quality audit, conditional cleanup, implication-direction backfill, PMXT/CLOB coverage expansion, `profit_le_0` audit

---

## Summary

This spike established the realistic ceiling for the February 22-27, 2026 PMXT backtest slice.

- The original conditional logic was manufacturing phantom arbitrage.
- Backfilling missing implication direction recovered the real executable trades.
- Price-data widening did not materially change outcomes.
- The remaining `profit_le_0` bucket is mostly correctly priced pairs, not a fixable logic bug.

**Final baseline for this window:** 90 executable opportunities, 180 paper trades, ending value **$10,001.35**.

---

## 1. Pair-Quality Audit

Initial audit on the isolated PMXT slice found:

- 370 detected opportunities
- 4 executable trades
- 366 optimizer rejects
- 205 rejects from `edge_sanity_cap`
- Lowering `min_edge` from `0.03` to `0.005` recovered **zero** additional executable trades

Conclusion: `min_edge` was not the bottleneck. The main problems were dependency labeling and price coverage.

Artifacts:

- `scripts/audit_pair_quality.py`

## 2. Conditional Cleanup

Two fixes were applied:

1. Reclassified obvious `conditional` pairs:
   - 158 `conditional -> implication` with direction
   - 1,369 `conditional -> none`
   - 825 remained `conditional`
2. Disabled the positive-correlation conditional infeasibility rule in `services/detector/constraints.py`

Impact:

- `edge_sanity_cap`: **1,435 -> 0**
- Executable opportunities: **43 -> 4**
- Paper trades: **86 -> 8**
- Final value: **$10,004.37 -> $10,004.23**

Interpretation: the prior 43 executables were fake arb caused by circular conditional constraints. After cleanup, only genuine implication opportunities remained.

Artifacts:

- `scripts/reclassify_conditional_pairs.py`
- `services/detector/constraints.py`

---

## 3. Implication Direction Backfill

Backfilled `implication_direction` for legacy implication pairs using rule-based matchers.

- 3,934 / 4,234 previously direction-less implication pairs filled
- Coverage: **92.9%**

Impact versus the post-conditional baseline:

- Executable opportunities: **4 -> 90**
- Total detections: **7 -> 367**
- Paper trades: **8 -> 180**
- `verification_fail`: **~3,107 -> ~700**
- Final value: **$10,004.23 -> $10,001.35**

Interpretation: this was the main remaining logic fix. It recovered real executable trades, even though marked-to-market PnL stayed modest because the window is short and many positions remained open.

Artifacts:

- `scripts/backfill_implication_direction.py`

---

## 4. Coverage Audit And Data Widening

Coverage audit on verified pairs showed PMXT sparsity was broad, not concentrated:

- 6,456 markets in verified pairs
- 5,090 markets with zero PMXT data (**78.8%**)
- 4,218 / 5,285 pairs always blocked across all 5 days
- 92.8% of `no_prices` cases were "both markets missing"

Widening experiments:

1. More PMXT hours per day: negligible gain
2. Earlier PMXT dates: unavailable; archive starts on **2026-02-22**
3. CLOB `/prices-history`: many responses, but most candles landed outside the Feb window or came back with current-day timestamps

Net impact of widening:

- `no_prices`: **21,283 -> 21,100**
- Detected: **367 -> 378**
- Executable opportunities: **90 -> 90**
- Paper trades: **180 -> 180**
- Final value: **$10,001.35 -> $10,001.35**

Interpretation: data widening did not unlock new trades. This window hit the practical coverage ceiling.

Artifacts:

- `scripts/audit_pmxt_coverage.py`
- `scripts/widen_pmxt_prices.py`
- `scripts/clob_backfill_uncovered.py`

## 5. Profit-Le-Zero Audit

Audit of the remaining `profit_le_0` pair-day evaluations:

- Total: **4,354**
- Implication: **3,815** (87.6%)
- Positive conditional: **504** (11.6%)
- Negative conditional: **35** (0.8%)

Distance from arbitrage boundary:

- Structural zero: **1,219** (28.0%)
- Sub-0.5% near-miss: **1,314** (30.2%)
- 0.5-1%: **128** (2.9%)
- 1-5%: **649** (14.9%)
- 5-15%: **1,100** (25.3%)
- >15%: **1,145** (26.3%)

Interpretation:

- Positive conditionals are correctly zero by design
- Most implication near-misses are one-tick differences on illiquid markets
- Those near-misses remain below meaningful execution thresholds
- The rest are correctly priced pairs, not missed arbitrage

Conclusion: `profit_le_0` is not a productive next target for this window.

Artifact:

- `scripts/audit_profit_le_0.py`

## Final Decision

This February 22-27, 2026 isolated PMXT spike is exhausted.

- No more threshold tuning
- No more conditional logic work
- No more implication-direction rule work on this slice
- No more PMXT/CLOB widening for this slice

The realistic yield for this window is:

- **90 executable opportunities**
- **180 paper trades**
- **$10,001.35 ending value**

Follow-up validation on March 20-25, 2026 confirmed that these logic fixes generalize when price coverage exists; see `reports/pmxt-mar20-25-validation-2026-03-27.md`.

The next meaningful experiment should use a different time window or a larger replay scope, not further optimization of this one.
